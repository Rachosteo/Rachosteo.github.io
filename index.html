About Rachael


Rachael graduated from the Univeristy College of Osteopathy, formerly the British School of Osteopathy, with a Masters in Osteopathy. She is registered with the General Osteopathic Council (GOsC) and is also a member of the Institute of Osteopathy (iO).

She has experience working in a variety of clinics, including the Royal Free hospital and alongside sports teams in the U.K and abroad. Every year, she attends courses and lectures on a wide range of osteopathic subjects to further this experience.

Rachael uses a combination of massage, manipulation, trigger point therapy and medical acupunture in her treatments.

Can Osteopathy help me?


Osteopathy treats a wide-range of ailments, below is a list of just a few:

  • Low back pain
  • Neck pain
  • Arthritic pain
  • Hip & knee pain
  • Headaches
  • Shoulder problems
  • Tennis elbow
  • Nerve pain, including sciatica
  • Sports injuries


If you suffer from any of the above conditions, or from something you feel an osteopath could help with, don't hesitate to give Rachael a call.
