About Rachael


Rachael graduated from the British School of Osteopathy with a Masters in Osteopathy and is registered with the General Osteopathic Council (GOsC). She is also a member of the Institute of Osteopathy (iO).

Rachael has experience working in the Royal Free Hospital clinic, working alongside the elderly and other speciality patients. Every year, she attends courses and lectures on a wide range of osteopathic subjects.

Rachael uses mainly structural techniques such as massage and manipulation, but also uses sports taping and medical acupuncture when found to be appropriate.


Can Osteopathy help me?


Osteopathy could help you if you suffer from:

  • Low back pain
  • Neck pain
  • Arthritic pain
  • Hip & knee pain
  • Headaches
  • Shoulder problems
  • Tennis elbow
  • Nerve pain, including sciatica
  • Sports injuries


If you suffer from any of the above conditions then contact us to book an initial consultation where we can further assess your needs.
